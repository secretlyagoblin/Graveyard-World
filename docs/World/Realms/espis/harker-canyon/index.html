Harker Canyon

Harker canyon is the shallowest part of the the Scar, the crossing of which is the last leg of the safest route from New Haven to the lesser-settled southern parts of Espis. Named after Neshum Harker, the Ranger who made the trail five years after the refounding of the realm.
