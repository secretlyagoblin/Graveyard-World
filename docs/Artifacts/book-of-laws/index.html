Book of Laws

Rebound by Recrentius in the Library of the God of Bloody Light, the book of Laws allows those who are following the righteous path laid out between its pages to gain insight and direction. Though the laws are arcane and open to interpretation, when properly attuned with ones god they will not lead you wrong.
